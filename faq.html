Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about my services

What services do you offer?

I offer comprehensive web development services including graphic design, product design, UI/UX design, and full-stack web development. This covers everything from initial concept and design to development, deployment, and ongoing maintenance.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website typically takes 2–4 weeks, while more complex applications can take 6–12 weeks or longer. During our initial consultation, I'll provide a detailed timeline based on your specific requirements.

What is your pricing structure?

Pricing depends on project scope, complexity, and timeline. I offer both fixed-price projects and hourly rates. After understanding your requirements, I'll provide a detailed quote with transparent pricing. Contact me for a free consultation and quote.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes, I work with clients both locally in Lagos and remotely from anywhere in the world. I use modern collaboration tools and communication platforms to ensure smooth project delivery regardless of location.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All websites I develop are fully responsive and optimized for all devices — smartphones, tablets, and desktops. I use a mobile-first approach to ensure excellent user experience across all screen sizes.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes, I offer various support and maintenance packages to keep your website running smoothly. This includes updates, security patches, bug fixes, and technical support. We can discuss the best support plan for your needs.

Can you help with website hosting and domain?

Yes, I can help you choose the right hosting solution and assist with domain registration. I'll guide you through the process and ensure your website is properly configured and deployed to a reliable hosting environment.

What if I need changes after the project is complete?

I offer a revision period after project completion for minor adjustments. For major changes or new features, we can discuss additional work as a new project phase or include it in an ongoing maintenance agreement.

Do you use templates or custom code?

I primarily build custom websites from scratch to ensure your site is unique and tailored to your specific needs. This approach provides better performance, flexibility, and SEO optimization compared to template-based solutions.

How do I get started with a project?

Simply contact me through the contact form, email, or WhatsApp. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project requirements, goals, and timeline. From there, I'll provide a detailed proposal and we can begin the exciting journey of bringing your vision to life!

Still have questions?

Feel free to reach out — I'm here to help!
